# Early Users

## User 1 — The Power User (Alex)

- Signed up in week 1, uses the product daily
- Found you through a Reddit post in r/{{domain}}
- Sends feature requests via email (2-3 per week)
- Has already hit edge cases you didn't anticipate
- Wants API access "so I can integrate this with my other tools"
- Would probably pay — but you haven't asked

## User 2 — The Silent User (Jordan)

- Signed up, completed setup, used the product twice, then went quiet
- You don't know why they stopped — no error logs, no feedback
- Their use case seems slightly different from what you designed for
- Represents the "churn before payment" problem

## User 3 — The Skeptic (Sam)

- Signed up after seeing your landing page, poked around for 10 minutes
- Left a one-line feedback: "looks interesting but I can't figure out how to {{domain}}"
- Hasn't logged in since
- Represents the onboarding gap — the product works, but the first-run experience doesn't guide people

## Patterns

- All 3 found you organically (no paid acquisition)
- None have been asked to pay yet
- You have no analytics to see what they actually did in the product
- The only feedback channel is email — no in-app mechanism
